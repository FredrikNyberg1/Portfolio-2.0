Lunchpad

Lunch system

Lunchpad was made due to a request by the principal of NTI-gymnasiet Uppsala. They wanted a system to ensure that students of the school don't cheat their specified lunch times.
Our solution was to use a Raspberry Pi connected to a screen and an NFC scanner at the entrance of the dining area running a Python program. The students are required to scan their keycards, and the screen shows whether it's currently their time to eat or not.
See it on GitHub here.

  • Client: NTI-Gymnasiet Uppsala
  • Category: Software design and development

Energiportalen

Website

A website that I was tasked with maintaining and working on during my time at Stuns Energi. The website is for displaying all the data regarding solar energy production around Uppsala, Sweden. It uses HTML, CSS, Graphana and React. It features live data display of solar energy, current wind speed and more. You can also now sort the different facilities of solar energy production based of different parameters as well as compare one facility to others.
You can see the site here.

  • Client: Region Uppsala
  • Category: Web design and development - Data display and management
