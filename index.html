Career

So far

  • 2017 - 2020

    Education and Graduation

    I graduated college from NTI Gymnasiet Uppsala in: "Tech, information and media technology". After graduating I studied an extra year to become a College engineer. The extra year is laidout to be similar to a workplace, no classes just working on projects in different groups.

  • Early 2021

    College engineer and internships

    During my time studying to college engineer I got to work with the company Stoqup on a landing page and with Stuns on their website "Energiportalen Region Uppsala". Both of these, along with other projects, can be seen in the section above.

  • Summer 2021

    Webdesign Stuns

    After graduation I got the oppertunity to work more with Stuns. This time on a data display map "Stuns Streetmaps" alongside with maintinance on "Energiportalen Region Uppsala". The map website is now being worked on by others but was built from the ground up by me and another colleague.

  • 2021 - Current

    Instrument Test Engineer, Quality Control

    After getting scouted I now work for Q-Linea as a Instrument Test Engineer in Quality Control. Maintaining, running tests and perfecting methods for the ASTar instrument.
